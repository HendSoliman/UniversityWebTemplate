ABOUT US

The AXIT University is one of the country's top-tier research universities. Our 158 buildings house more than 200 academic majors, more than 2,000 faculty and instructional staff, and some 11,100 students—approximately half of whom are women...

A collection of driven, friendly students. Professors dedicated to teaching them. A stunning campus that brings people together again and again. Remarkable alumni. That's AXIT..

AXIT alumni go on to careers in academia, medicine, law, music, finance, education and numerous other fields. They are surgeons, actors, legal advocates and environmental activists.
